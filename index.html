Spring Break 2027

Chasing Cherry Blossoms

26 Mar - 07 Apr Seoul & Jeju

To-Do List

Don't forget to book domestic flights from Seoul Gimpo (GMP) to Jeju (CJU) for Mar 29, and return for Apr 2. Also, secure a rental car in Jeju!

Part 1

Hello, Seoul!

Fri, 26 Mar

Arrival in Seoul

  • 3:40 PM: Arrive at Incheon (ICN). Clear customs, pick up your pre-booked eSIM/portable Wi-Fi, and load cash onto T-Money transit cards at the convenience store. Take the AREX express train to Seoul Station.
  • 5:30 PM: Check into your hotel. Freshen up after the long journey.
  • 7:00 PM: Welcome dinner! Grab authentic Korean BBQ in Myeongdong, or try the Michelin-recognized garlic dumplings at Myeongdong Kyoja. Afterwards, stroll the Myeongdong Night Market for street food snacks (hotteok, cheese lobster, and egg bread!).
Sat, 27 Mar

Palaces & Traditions

  • 9:30 AM: Gyeongbokgung Palace. Rent a Hanbok nearby for free entry and amazing photos around the pavilions. Catch the Royal Guard Changing Ceremony.
  • 2:00 PM: Stroll through Bukchon Hanok Village (remember to keep voices low) and Insadong for traditional tea and crafts.
  • 4:30 PM: Rest your feet at Ikseon-dong Hanok Village. This maze of narrow alleys is packed with Seoul's trendiest aesthetic cafes, craft breweries, and artisan boutiques.
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner at Gwangjang Market. Grab a stool and try Bindaetteok (mung bean pancakes), Mayak Gimbap, and fresh dumplings.
  • 8:30 PM: Walk off dinner with a peaceful evening stroll along the beautifully illuminated Cheonggyecheon Stream, located just a short walk from the market.
Sun, 28 Mar

Views & Culture

  • 10:00 AM: Visit the spectacular National Museum of Korea. The architecture is stunning. Don't miss the picturesque Yongsan Family Park right next door.
  • 1:30 PM: Head up to the Itaewon / Hannam-dong area. This is a great spot for international cuisine, followed by browsing trendy Korean flagship streetwear brands like Mardi Mercredi.
  • 5:00 PM: Sunset at N Seoul Tower via the Namsan Cable Car. Look out for early spring blooms in Namsan Park! For dinner, try the famous "Namsan Tonkatsu" (giant pork cutlet) near the cable car station.
Part 2

Jeju Island

Fly south for volcanic landscapes, coastal drives, and peak Jeju Cherry Blossoms.

Mon, 29 Mar

Hello, Jeju!

  • Morning: Fly from Seoul Gimpo (GMP) to Jeju (CJU) and pick up your pre-booked rental car.
  • 11:30 AM: Drive through Jeonnong-ro Cherry Blossom Street. Late March is peak bloom in Jeju, and this is the most famous spot!
  • 2:00 PM: Walk the Aewol Handam Coastal Trail. Grab a coastal coffee at Cafe Knotted. If time permits, drive 20 mins down to Hyeopjae Beach for striking emerald waters and white sand.
  • 7:00 PM: Head to Dongmun Night Market. The vibe is electric with fire-torched street food, abalone kimbap, and fresh citrus juices.
Tue, 30 Mar

West & South Wonders

  • 9:30 AM: Visit Osulloc Tea Museum & Innisfree Jeju House. Enjoy matcha green tea rolls and views of the sprawling tea fields.
  • 12:00 PM: See Sanbangsan Mountain, surrounded by bright yellow canola flowers. If the tide is low, take the stunning walk along the Yongmeori Coast at the mountain's base.
  • 4:00 PM: View Cheonjiyeon Waterfall in Seogwipo. Afterwards, grab an early dinner at the Seogwipo Maeil Olle Market (famous for garlic fried chicken).
Wed, 31 Mar

Sunrise Peak & East Coast

  • 9:00 AM: Hike Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak) for volcanic crater views. It's a short but steep stair climb!
  • 11:30 AM: Watch the famous Haenyeo (Women Divers) demonstration at the base of the peak.
  • 2:30 PM: Walk the stunning Seopjikoji coastal trails. Right next door is Aqua Planet Jeju, Asia's largest aquarium, perfect for a late afternoon visit.
Thu, 01 Apr

Udo Island Getaway

  • 9:00 AM: Take the short 15-minute ferry from Seongsan Port to Udo Island.
  • 10:30 AM: Rent electric bikes to circumnavigate the island. Stop at Hagosudong Beach, see the unique popcorn-shaped sand at Seobin Baeksa, and try the famous Udo Peanut Ice Cream!
  • 7:00 PM: Back on Jeju, treat yourselves to authentic Jeju Black Pork BBQ for dinner. A famous spot is Black Pork Street in Jeju City.
Part 3

Seoul in Full Bloom

Return to the capital just in time for the spectacular cherry blossom peak.

Fri, 02 Apr

Return to the Capital

  • 9:00 AM: Dongmun Market in Jeju for souvenirs (Hallabong orange chocolates and matcha goods).
  • Midday: Fly from Jeju (CJU) back to Seoul Gimpo (GMP).
  • 4:00 PM: Check into Seoul hotel. Visit Starfield COEX Mall & the iconic Starfield Library in Gangnam. Find peace at the historic Bongeunsa Temple across the street.
  • 7:30 PM: Head down the road to the Gangnam Station area. The streets behind the station are packed with bustling BBQ joints, pubs, and cafes.
Sat, 03 Apr

Cherry Blossom Festival

  • 10:00 AM: Yeouido Hangang Park. Walk the famous Yunjung-ro cherry blossom tunnels alongside thousands of locals!
  • 12:30 PM: Picnic by the Han River. Order 'Chimaek' (fried chicken and beer) delivery right to your picnic mat.
  • 2:30 PM: Walk over to The Hyundai Seoul, a spectacular department store featuring a massive indoor botanical garden and premium pop-ups.
  • 6:00 PM: Explore Hongdae for youthful energy, incredible K-pop street buskers, vintage shopping, and endless dinner options.
Sun, 04 Apr

Lake Blooms & City Views

  • 10:00 AM: Seokchon Lake Park. The lake is entirely surrounded by blooming cherry trees creating a massive pink ring.
  • 1:00 PM: Browse the luxury shops and grab lunch at the Lotte World Mall, then head up to the Seoul Sky Observatory for 360-degree panoramas of the sprawling city.
  • 4:30 PM: Take a short taxi ride to the vast Olympic Park. Rent a quad-bike to explore the art installations and visit the famous 'Lone Tree'.
Mon, 05 Apr

Trendy Cafes & Design

  • 10:00 AM: Seoul Forest Park. Enjoy blossoms, vibrant tulip fields, and feeding the deer.
  • 1:00 PM: Walk over to Seongsu-dong (the "Brooklyn of Seoul"). Famous for industrial-chic cafes like Onion and Daelim Changgo. Don't miss the Dior flagship popup.
  • 4:00 PM: Dongdaemun Design Plaza (DDP) for futuristic architecture by Zaha Hadid. Great at sunset!
  • 7:00 PM: For a unique dinner, head a few blocks over to Sindang-dong Tteokbokki Town. Order a massive pot of customized spicy rice cakes cooked right at your table.
Tue, 06 Apr

Last Minute Shopping

  • Morning: Free time. Optional: book a half-day tour to the DMZ (Demilitarized Zone).
  • 2:00 PM: Final shopping run in Myeongdong. Hit the massive Olive Young flagship store for K-beauty skincare hauls, or Namdaemun Market for cheaper, traditional goods and souvenirs.
  • 7:00 PM: Farewell Dinner! For the ultimate beef experience, head to the Majang Meat Market. Buy premium Hanwoo beef directly from the butchers and grill it at the restaurants upstairs.
Wed, 07 Apr

Departure

  • 7:30 AM: Head to Incheon International Airport (ICN) via the AREX train or airport limousine bus.
  • 9:00 AM: ICN has world-class duty-free shopping and excellent food courts. Grab a final bowl of Bibimbap or a pastry from Paris Baguette before heading to your gate.
  • 11:05 AM: Flight departs for Singapore. Have a safe trip back to Perth!
