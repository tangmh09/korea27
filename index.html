Spring Break 2027

Chasing Cherry Blossoms

13 Days exploring the historical streets of Seoul and the natural volcanic wonders of Jeju Island during peak spring bloom.

Dates

26 Mar - 07 Apr

Destinations

Seoul & Jeju

To-Do List

Don't forget to book your domestic flights from Seoul Gimpo (GMP) to Jeju (CJU) for Mar 29, and the return for Apr 2. Also, secure a rental car in Jeju in advance!

Part 1

Hello, Seoul!

Settle in and explore the historical heart of South Korea.

Fri, 26 Mar

Arrival in Seoul

  • 3:40 PM: Arrive at Incheon (ICN). Pick up a T-Money transport card at a convenience store and take the AREX train to the city.
  • 5:30 PM: Check into your hotel (Myeongdong or Hongdae recommended for first-timers).
  • 7:00 PM: Welcome dinner! Grab authentic Korean BBQ (Samgyeopsal) near your hotel.
  • 9:00 PM: Take a post-dinner stroll along the illuminated Cheonggyecheon Stream or browse the Myeongdong Night Market for street food desserts (Hotteok).
Sat, 27 Mar

Palaces & Traditions

  • 9:30 AM: Gyeongbokgung Palace. Rent a Hanbok nearby for free entry and catch the Royal Guard Changing Ceremony at 10:00 AM.
  • 12:30 PM: Walk past the serene Jogyesa Temple on your way to lunch.
  • 2:00 PM: Stroll the narrow, sloping alleys of Bukchon Hanok Village. Afterward, head down to Insadong for traditional tea and artisan crafts.
  • 6:30 PM: Dinner at Gwangjang Market. Sit at a vendor stall and try Bindaetteok (mung bean pancakes) and Mayak Gimbap.
Sun, 28 Mar

Views & Culture

  • 9:30 AM: Start at the War Memorial of Korea. An incredibly moving and expansive outdoor/indoor museum.
  • 12:00 PM: Walk or take a short cab to the National Museum of Korea to view millennia of Korean history.
  • 3:00 PM: Head up to Itaewon/Hannam-dong. Grab a late international lunch and explore the indie boutiques and cafes.
  • 6:00 PM: Take the Namsan Cable Car up to N Seoul Tower. Catch the sunset and see the city light up.
Part 2

Jeju Island

Fly south for volcanic landscapes, coastal drives, and peak cherry blossoms.

Mon, 29 Mar

Hello, Jeju!

  • Morning: Fly from Seoul Gimpo (GMP) to Jeju (CJU). Pick up your rental car at the airport.
  • 11:30 AM: Drive through Jeonnong-ro Cherry Blossom Street to see the island in full bloom.
  • 2:00 PM: Head west. Walk the Aewol Handam Coastal Trail. Grab coffee at Cafe Knotted or one of the ocean-view glass cafes.
  • 6:30 PM: Head back to Jeju City. Explore Dongmun Night Market for abalone kimbap, citrus juices, and street food for dinner.
Tue, 30 Mar

West & South Wonders

  • 9:30 AM: Drive to Osulloc Tea Museum. Walk the green tea fields and get a matcha roll cake at Innisfree Jeju House.
  • 12:30 PM: View Sanbangsan Mountain, heavily surrounded by bright yellow canola flowers during spring.
  • 2:00 PM: Hike down to Yongmeori Coast (Dragon Head Coast) to see spectacular layered volcanic rock formations right on the water.
  • 4:30 PM: Drive to Seogwipo. View the beautiful Cheonjiyeon Waterfall via a peaceful forested path.
  • 6:30 PM: Dinner and exploring at Seogwipo Maeil Olle Market. Try Jeju black pork skewers!
Wed, 31 Mar

Sunrise Peak & East Coast

  • 9:00 AM: Hike Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak). The 20-30 min stair climb rewards you with amazing volcanic crater views.
  • 11:30 AM: Head to the cove at the base of the peak to watch the legendary Haenyeo (Women Divers) singing and diving.
  • 2:00 PM: Walk the stunning Seopjikoji coastal trails, famous for its lighthouse and cliffside views.
  • 4:00 PM: Take a relaxing walk through Bijarim Forest, an ancient forest filled with nutmeg yew trees.
Thu, 01 Apr

Udo Island Getaway

  • 9:00 AM: Take the 15-minute car ferry from Seongsan Port to Udo Island.
  • 10:00 AM: Rent electric bikes or a sidecar to circumnavigate. Stop at Seobin Baeksa (popcorn sand beach) and Hagosudong Beach.
  • 1:00 PM: Grab lunch on the island and don't forget to try the famous Udo Peanut Ice Cream!
  • 4:30 PM: Take the ferry back. Do a scenic coastal drive along the northeast Jongdal-ri coastal road before sunset.
  • 7:00 PM: Treat yourselves to a sit-down, premium authentic Jeju Black Pork BBQ dinner.
Part 3

Seoul in Full Bloom

Return to the capital just in time for the spectacular cherry blossom peak.

Fri, 02 Apr

Return to the Capital

  • 9:00 AM: Dongmun Market in Jeju for souvenirs. Buy Hallabong orange chocolates and matcha goods.
  • Midday: Drop off the rental car and fly from Jeju (CJU) back to Seoul Gimpo (GMP).
  • 4:00 PM: Check into Seoul hotel. Head to Gangnam and visit the peaceful Bongeunsa Temple.
  • 5:30 PM: Cross the street to Starfield COEX Mall. Visit the massive, Instagram-famous Starfield Library.
Sat, 03 Apr

Cherry Blossom Festival

  • 10:00 AM: Yeouido Hangang Park. Walk the famous cherry blossom tunnels! This is the peak spring festival.
  • 11:30 AM: Rent Seoul city bikes (Ttareungi) and ride along the Han River paths.
  • 1:00 PM: Picnic by the river. Have delivery food dropped off or grab 'Chimaek' (chicken & beer) from convenience stores.
  • 5:00 PM: Take the subway to Hongdae. Walk the Gyeongui Line Forest Park in Yeonnam-dong (great for youth culture and cafes).
  • 7:30 PM: Explore the vibrant streets of Hongdae, watch street buskers, and enjoy dinner and shopping.
Sun, 04 Apr

Lake Blooms & Views

  • 10:00 AM: Seokchon Lake Park. The entire figure-eight lake is surrounded by densely packed blooming trees.
  • 12:00 PM: Explore the massive Lotte World Mall for lunch and window shopping.
  • 2:30 PM: Head up to the Seoul Sky Observatory (Lotte World Tower - the 6th tallest building in the world) for unbelievable 360-degree views.
  • 5:00 PM: Take a short ride to Olympic Park. Rent a tandem bike, find the famous 'Lone Tree', and enjoy a peaceful evening.
Mon, 05 Apr

Trendy Cafes & Design

  • 10:00 AM: Seoul Forest Park. Enjoy the last of the blossoms, tulip fields, and feed the deer.
  • 12:30 PM: Walk over to Seongsu-dong (the "Brooklyn of Seoul"). Hop between industrial-chic cafes like Cafe Onion or Grandpa Factory.
  • 4:00 PM: Take the subway to Dongdaemun Design Plaza (DDP) to admire Zaha Hadid's futuristic architecture.
  • 7:00 PM: Eat dinner in the Dongdaemun area (famous for Dakhanmari - whole chicken soup) and check out the late-night wholesale fashion markets.
Tue, 06 Apr

Last Minute Shopping

  • 10:00 AM: Namdaemun Market. Have Kalguksu (knife-cut noodles) in the famous noodle alley and buy traditional souvenirs.
  • 1:00 PM: Visit the Lotte Department Store basement food hall in Myeongdong for premium snacks, teas, and treats to take home.
  • 3:00 PM: Final shopping run in Myeongdong to stock up on K-Beauty and skincare products.
  • 7:30 PM: Farewell Dinner! Treat yourselves to high-end Korean Hanwoo Beef or a traditional Hanjeongsik (multi-course meal).
Wed, 07 Apr

Departure

  • 7:30 AM: Check out and head to Incheon International Airport (ICN) via the AREX Express Train.
  • 9:00 AM: Drop bags, clear security, and grab one final Korean meal (like Jjigae) at the airport food court.
  • 11:05 AM: Flight SQ 611 departs for Singapore. Safe travels back home!
