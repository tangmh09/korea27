Spring Break 2027

Chasing Cherry Blossoms

13 Days exploring the historical streets of Seoul and the natural volcanic wonders of Jeju Island during peak spring bloom.

Dates

26 Mar - 07 Apr

Destinations

Seoul & Jeju

To-Do List

Don't forget to book your domestic flights from Seoul Gimpo (GMP) to Jeju (CJU) for Mar 29, and the return for Apr 2. Also, secure a rental car in Jeju in advance!

Part 1

Hello, Seoul!

Settle in and explore the historical heart of South Korea.

1
Fri, 26 Mar

Arrival in Seoul

  • 3:40 PM: Arrive at Incheon (ICN). Take the AREX express train to the city.
  • 5:30 PM: Check into your hotel (Myeongdong or Hongdae recommended).
  • 7:00 PM: Welcome dinner! Grab Korean BBQ and explore Myeongdong Night Market.
2
Sat, 27 Mar

Palaces & Traditions

  • 9:30 AM: Gyeongbokgung Palace. Rent a Hanbok nearby for free entry.
  • 2:00 PM: Stroll through Bukchon Hanok Village and Insadong.
  • 7:00 PM: Dinner at Gwangjang Market (Try Bindaetteok).
3
Sun, 28 Mar

Views & Culture

  • 10:00 AM: Visit the spectacular National Museum of Korea.
  • 1:30 PM: Head to Itaewon/Hannam-dong for an late lunch and cafe hopping.
  • 5:00 PM: N Seoul Tower for sunset views over the city.
Part 2

Jeju Island

Fly south for volcanic landscapes, coastal drives, and peak cherry blossoms.

4
Mon, 29 Mar

Hello, Jeju!

  • Morning: Fly from Seoul Gimpo (GMP) to Jeju (CJU) & get rental car.
  • 11:30 AM: Drive through Jeonnong-ro Cherry Blossom Street.
  • 2:00 PM: Walk the Aewol Handam Coastal Trail and grab coffee.
5
Tue, 30 Mar

West & South Wonders

  • 9:30 AM: Osulloc Tea Museum & Innisfree Jeju House.
  • 12:00 PM: See Sanbangsan Mountain, surrounded by canola flowers.
  • 4:00 PM: View Cheonjiyeon Waterfall in Seogwipo.
6
Wed, 31 Mar

Sunrise Peak & East Coast

  • 9:00 AM: Hike Seongsan Ilchulbong (Sunrise Peak) for views.
  • 11:30 AM: Watch the famous Haenyeo (Women Divers) nearby.
  • 2:30 PM: Walk the stunning Seopjikoji coastal trails.
7
Thu, 01 Apr

Udo Island Getaway

  • 9:00 AM: Take the short ferry from Seongsan Port to Udo Island.
  • 10:30 AM: Rent electric bikes to circumnavigate. Try Udo Peanut Ice Cream!
  • 7:00 PM: Back on Jeju, treat yourselves to authentic Jeju Black Pork BBQ.
Part 3

Seoul in Full Bloom

Return to the capital just in time for the spectacular cherry blossom peak.

8
Fri, 02 Apr

Return to the Capital

  • 9:00 AM: Dongmun Market in Jeju for souvenirs.
  • Midday: Fly from Jeju (CJU) back to Seoul Gimpo (GMP).
  • 4:00 PM: Check into Seoul hotel. Visit Starfield COEX Mall.
9
Sat, 03 Apr

Cherry Blossom Festival

  • 10:00 AM: Yeouido Hangang Park. Walk the famous tunnels!
  • 12:30 PM: Picnic by the Han River. Order 'Chimaek' (chicken & beer).
  • 6:00 PM: Explore Hongdae for youthful energy and shopping.
10
Sun, 04 Apr

Lake Blooms & Views

  • 10:00 AM: Seokchon Lake Park. Surrounded by blooming trees.
  • 2:30 PM: Head up Seoul Sky Observatory (Lotte World Tower).
11
Mon, 05 Apr

Trendy Cafes & Design

  • 10:00 AM: Seoul Forest Park. Enjoy blossoms and tulip fields.
  • 1:00 PM: Cafe hopping in Seongsu-dong ("Brooklyn of Seoul").
  • 4:00 PM: Dongdaemun Design Plaza (DDP).
12
Tue, 06 Apr

Last Minute Shopping

  • Morning: Free time or optional half-day DMZ tour.
  • 2:00 PM: Final shopping run in Myeongdong or Namdaemun.
  • 7:30 PM: Farewell Dinner! Treat yourselves to Korean Hanwoo Beef.
13
Wed, 07 Apr

Departure

  • 7:30 AM: Head to Incheon International Airport (ICN).
